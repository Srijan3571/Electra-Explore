GYMKHANA:


Gymkhana Students’ Union Body is the official body to look after overall student activities at NIT Silchar. The members are officially elected after a proper election system in the institute itself by the whole student fraternity of NIT Silchar.
• Gymkhana Council coordinates all the activities of the Gymkhana such as framing the budget, exercising financial control, instituting, and declaring awards.
• It forms and monitor any sub-committees, clubs/shells or societies or disband the existing ones and when necessary with the approval of the competent authority.
• It has three major divisions e.g., cultural committee, sports committee, and hostel affairs committee.
• The GC, towards the end of its tenure, prepares an annual report of its activities accounts, etc.

1. CULTURAL

a. Drama: Drama is an art, a way of life. Acknowledging its worth, the dramatics club, NIT Silchar. Came into being. The prime objective of this club is the exposure of the natural acting talent of the students. And to up bring the quality of dramatics and dance in the same.
b. Dance: Dance is an emotion that helps us to express thousands of thoughts through our moves. This club sees after the dance modules of various genres of the institute. It also organises workshops on various dance forms throughout the year.
c. Film society: This society looks after the cinematography aspect of the institute.
d. Literary publications and fine arts society: This society is for all those who are good in literature, story writing, debate, content writing, and so on. This allows you to show your talents in public speaking and mentoring.
e. Music: All the enthusiastic singers and musicians are welcome here.

2. GENERAL FRESHER:


All of you must be well excited about your general fresher’s. Our college conducts one general freshers for all the students in which you can also take part as a participant in music, dance, drama, or any other performance. There are also the titles for Mr. Fresher and Miss Fresher. If you want to participate in that, then you must go through the various rounds for the selection procedure. After the selection, a few of you will be selected based on your talents and Ramp-Walk skills. Don’t worry, you will be taught the Ramp-Walk beforehand. The judges will judge you based on your confidence and public speaking also. And the selected candidates will get a chance to perform on the stage in front of the entire college. This is a very exciting event so don’t hesitate to take part in it.

3. INDUCTION:

For the welcoming of a new batch and getting started with them, the institute conducts an induction programme of a few days. There are various events organised under the induction programme, as well as various competitions. Chess, carrom, tennis, etc all are conducted. The motto for the induction is for the students to better understand the college and get interacted with their fellow mates with whom they will be spending 4 years of their college life.

VARIOUS CLUBS


1. Robotics Club:


With the exponential growth in the influence of robots in the corporate and industrial worlds, NIT Silchar is also on its way to leaving its footprint on the innovator community. N.E.R.D.S. trains students in the domain of robotics and embedded systems and makes them the world-class innovators of tomorrow.

2. Coding club:


Almost everything today runs on code. Coding is one of the most essential skills for an engineer. With the same outlook in mind, the coding club strives to teach students to code. It trains students in the commonest languages from beginner to advanced levels. NIT Silchar proudly flaunts some of the best coders in the nation

3. Skating club:


This comes under sports. This club teaches you skating and helps you to become a master in skating.

4. Dance club(NSD):


:NSD or NIT Silchar Dance Club club is one of the most prominent clubs of NIT Silchar with over 50 dancers associated with it and numerous other dance enthusiasts. The main events falling under NSD are Flashmob, the promotional event of Incandescence, and the representation of NIT Silchar in Springfest IIT Kharagpur. Every year the club nurtures people who participate in the Dance Workshop for all alike. Apart from this NSD began the culture of Let's Nacho and Talent Hunt to make people leave their comfort zones with ease.

5. Drama club(Advay):


" There are more things on Heaven and Earth, Horatio; than are dreamt of in your philosophy. " What is life without a little drama? Advay is the place for you if you express yourself with your expressions. Let the spark within you run wild, and set the stage on fire. Advay trains the students in the field of dramatics and teaches them to harness their emotions and express them in front of the spotlight.

6. Music club:


NIT Silchar is a place where talents of all kinds are appreciated. Where our academics demand us to be precise as engineers, the music club of NIT Silchar is where you can let your imagination run free. For the musicians, the music club organizes regular training and jamming sessions to shape the students into better musicians.

7. NITS Eco-club:


We have done enough harm to our mother nature,Eco Club NITS is a step by the students of NIT Silchar to Compensate for the harm and descry ways to heal our ailing nature with a team of high spirits and extreme enthusiasts who are willing to move heaven and earth for saving the Environment.

8. Mountaineering And Trekking club:


NIT Silchar allows you to go on an adventure with your mates on behalf of the mountaineering and trekking club. It helps and nurtures the growing interest of mountain climbers and those who want to challenge the ever so mysterious terrains of the mountains. But it also demands highly active students who are ready to take the heat.

9. Google Developers Student Club(GDSC):


Almost everything today runs on code. Coding is one of the most essential skills for an engineer. With the same outlook in mind, the coding club strives to teach students to code. It trains students in the commonest languages from beginner to advanced levels. NIT Silchar proudly flaunts some of the best coders in the nation.

10. Machine Learning club:


Artificial Intelligence is no longer something only limited to the cinematic universe. With every passing day, it is developing too rapidly. The Machine Learning Club of NIT Silchar aspires to keep up with this trend and teach the students the basics of this evolving technology

11. E-Cell NIT Silchar:


E-Cell, NIT Silchar or Entrepreneurship Cell of the National Institute of Technology, Silchar is a non-profit organization run by the students of NIT Silchar established to promote entrepreneurship and start-ups within the college. Keeping an aim of fostering and nurturing the spirit of entrepreneurship in the youth of NIT Silchar, we at E-Cell NIT Silchar have conceptualized and successfully implemented various initiatives to help students, young entrepreneurs, and professionals in their entrepreneurial journey.

12. ASME:


ASME NIT Silchar Chapter is the youngest technical community of NIT Silchar. It aims to promote the art, science, and practice of multidisciplinary engineering and allied sciences among the NIT fraternity. The chapter conducts a plethora of events ranging from design competitions, and periodic webinars from dignified guests along with participation in the highly coveted ASME E-Fest Asia Pacific that lets students discover a plethora of fields like Mechatronics, Composites, Machine Design, etc.

13. Gyansagar:


With all the technical and non-technical clubs and societies meant solely for the students of the institution, Gyansagar is in turn an initiative of the students of the institution to give back to society. We at NIT Silchar believe that every small step matters. Cyansagar carries out donation drives and provides free education facilities to the needy inside and around the campus.

14. Obiettivo:


With a page reach of almost 13,000, Obiettivo is one of the most successful clubs amongst the NITS. Obiettivo is responsible for capturing all the important moments in the institution. From the fresher's party to farewell, Obiettivo has it all covered. Obiettivo has been the home to both globally and nationally recognized photographers.

15. ILLUMINITS:


Illuminits provides a relaxing and interactive environment while giving the students a platform to hone their fine arts and literary skills both in national and vernacular languages. Every year, Illuminits grooms individuals and helps them overcome their fear of literary feats, boosting their confidence be it speaking skills, creative writing, journalism, quizzing, design or arts so they may leave the college as professional and trained individuals whom the corporate world demands

16.NITSMUN:


:NITSMUN is the MUN Society of the National Institute of Technology, Silchar, an Institute of National Importance as per the Government of India. NITSMUN is a forerunner in the MUN circuit of North East India and, has been privileged enough to achieve collaboration with some of the finest names of India's MUN circle. Since its inception in 2014, NITSMUN has been successfully hosting Annual MUN Conferences, workshops, and sessions, inculcating in youth the skills of public speaking, thorough research, qualitative debating, communication skills, and judgment 2acuity. Together with these, NITSMUN has also sent participations to numerous MUN Conferences, bringing honor to the institute and hence has evolved from a pioneer to a leader.

SPORTS

1.Football:Football is a sport that excites many talented students which allows them to play in Inter-NIT to represent our college. There is a football ground in which you can practice and play on your own. In the Inter-Hostel competition also, football plays a part.
2. Basketball: Though not so famous but NIT Silchar allows you to learn to play basketball and represent our college in Inter-NIT as well. You all are highly welcome on the basketball court and show your talents.
3. Cricket: The sport which everyone wants to play and most of us play in our localities is cricket. Many of you will have a dream to play cricket to get acknowledged, so there it is. The cricket ground is a place where you can play whenever you want and enjoy it. If you are talented then you can try a chance in the INTER-NIT!! Never miss a chance.
4. Lawn Tennis: Many of you have only maybe heard of it but now you can get to play it as well in the fully furnished Tennis court which gives you exposure to bring out your talent.

1. Indoor games


Well, who doesn’t love indoor games? Hard work and easy to showcase talents. But do not consider this as an easy task, because the competition will be high. In the SAC building, you are allowed to play many indoor games of your choice
a. Table Tennis: If not lawn tennis then table tennis! You can play whatever you want and also take part in Inter-NIT.
b. Chess: Where are the masterminds? This is your place to knock your heads and grab the opportunity to be the champion. The competition in chess is tough but don’t get disappointed. Practice makes a man perfect, so get up and come to practice in the SAC building in the evening hours. You will learn a lot.
c.Badminton:: One of the most loved sport. Everyone wants to make a try in badminton, so I can expect you got the hint that getting an empty court is not so easy. Be there, wait for your turn and play. There are plenty of competitions for you, single, doubles as well as mixed doubles. Get a partner and get started.
d. Carrom: There are carrom setups as well which keeps the students entertained.

2. Kabbadi and Kho-Kho


Did you use to be a KABBADI champion in your school/college? Don’t worry, you will get the chance to be one in NIT Silchar as well. You will be given the chance to play in the Inter-NIT. And if you know nothing about kabbadi, you are still welcome. The seniors will teach you how to play and keep you under their observation with a strict routine and a good amount of practice. Lots of practice and then you will see wonders. The college team is a well-crafted and rigorously trained team on an amateur level, consisting of good raiders, defenders, and all-rounders. The freshmen students are taught about the basics of the games right from the beginning with practice matches being held at regular intervals to check the growth of the students in the sports.

3. ATHLETICS AND GYM


The fitness freaks should pay attention to the well-furnished gym on the campus. This club is responsible for conducting fitness events like bodybuilding competition, powerlifting, and arm wrestling along with athletics events like sprinting, marathon run, javelin throw, shot put throw, discus throw, and cycling; throughout the year. Recently, this club has started Yoga training on the campus which attracted a lot of participation.
